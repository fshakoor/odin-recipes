Fish and Rice

Description

A simple fish & rice recipe for beginners using basic ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 250 grams fish pieces
  • 3/4 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • tumeric powder as needed
  • salt as needed
  • lemon juice as needed
  • 2 sprigs curry leaves
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • 1 to 2 tbsp ghee
  • 2 to 3 green chilies
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 onion

Steps

  1. Wash, soak and cook rice al dente
  2. Marinate fish and set aside for 10 minutes
  3. Fry garlic in hot ghee and add curry leaves and fish. Fry for 3 min and flip. Add more spice powders as needed
  4. Fry till done and transfer to plate
  5. Add more ghee to pan
  6. Fry onions and green chili until golden
  7. Add curry leaves and saute
  8. Add cooled rice salt and pepper
  9. Serve rice and fish with fresh salad or sliced onions and carrots
